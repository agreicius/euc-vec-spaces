Definition 4.1.10.

Let \(A\in M_{mn}\text{.}\) The null space of \(A\text{,}\) denoted \(\NS A\text{,}\) is the set of all solutions to the matrix equation
\begin{equation} A\boldx=\underset{m\times 1}{\boldzero}\text{.}\tag{4.1.1} \end{equation}
In other words,
\begin{equation*} \NS A=\{\boldx\in \R^n\colon A\boldx=\boldzero\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Equivalently, thinking in terms of linear systems, \(\NS A\) is the set of solutions to the homogeneous linear system represented by (4.1.1).
