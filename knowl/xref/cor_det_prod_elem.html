Corollary 3.5.23. Determinant and products of elementary matrices.

Let \(A\) be an \(n\times n\) matrix, and suppose we have
\begin{equation} B=E_rE_{r-1}\cdots E_1\, A\tag{3.5.11} \end{equation}
for some collection of elementary matrices \(E_i\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation} \det B=\det E_r\, \det E_{r-1}\cdots \det E_1\, \det A\text{.}\tag{3.5.12} \end{equation}

Proof.
