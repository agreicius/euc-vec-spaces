Theorem 4.5.7. Null space and image as fundamental spaces.

Let \(A\) be an \(m\times n\) matrix, and let \(T_A\colon \R^n\rightarrow \R^m\) be its corresponding matrix transformation. The following equalities hold:
\begin{align} \NS A\amp=\NS T_A \tag{4.5.3}\\ \CS A\amp=\im T_A \tag{4.5.4}\\ \CS A \amp= \{\boldy\in \R^m\colon A\boldx=\boldy \text{ is consistent}\} \text{.}\tag{4.5.5} \end{align}
