Theorem 5.3.11. Coordinate vector transformation.

Let \(B=(\boldv_1,\boldv_2,\dots, \boldv_n)\) be an ordered basis for the vector space \(V\text{.}\) The function
\begin{align*} T\colon V \amp \rightarrow \R^n\\ \boldv \amp \mapsto [\boldv]_B \end{align*}
is an isomorphism: i.e., \(T\) is an invertible linear transformation.
