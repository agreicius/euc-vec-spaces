Corollary 6.1.16. Eigenvalues of a matrix.

Let \(A\) be an \(n\times n\) matrix, let \(p(t)=\det(tI-A)\text{,}\) and let \(\lambda\in \R\text{.}\) The following are equivalent:
  1. \(\lambda\) is an eigenvalue of \(A\text{;}\)
  2. \(\lambda I-A\) is singular;
  3. \(\det(\lambda I-A)=0\text{;}\)
  4. \(p(\lambda)=0\text{.}\)
