Example 2.1.7. Visualizing planes in \(\R^3\).

Let \(\mathcal{P}\subseteq \R^3\) be a plane defined by the nontrivial equation
\begin{equation} ax+by+cz=d\text{.}\tag{2.3} \end{equation}
