\begin{equation*} T_1(v) = 7 v + 4 u, \ \ \ T_1(u) = -5 v - 7 u, \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} T_2(v) = -4 v + 4 u, \ \ \ T_2(u) = -6 v - 4 u. \end{equation*}
Find the images of \(v\) and \(u\) under the composite of \(T_1\) and \(T_2\text{.}\)
\((T_2 T_1)(v) =\) ,
\((T_2 T_1)(u) =\) .
Answer 1.
\(-52v+12u\)
Answer 2.
\(62v+8u\)
