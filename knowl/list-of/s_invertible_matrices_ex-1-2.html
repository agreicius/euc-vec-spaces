Exercise 1.
If \(A\) and \(B\) are invertible \(n\times n\) matrices, then the inverse of \(A+B\) is \(A^{-1}+B^{-1}\text{.}\)
  • True
  • False
Answer.
\(\text{False}\)
Solution.
SOLUTION: False. For example, let \(A = I_n\text{,}\) and \(B = -I_n\text{,}\) then \(A+B = 0_n\text{,}\) which is not invertible.
