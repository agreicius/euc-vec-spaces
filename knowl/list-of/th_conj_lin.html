Theorem 5.1.16. Conjugation is linear.

Let \(n\) be a positive integer, and let \(Q\in M_{nn}\) be a fixed invertible matrix. The conjugation by \(Q\) function
\begin{align*} T\colon M_{nn} \amp \rightarrow M_{nn}\\ A \amp \mapsto Q^{-1}AQ \end{align*}
is a linear transformation.

Proof.

The proof is left as an exercise.
