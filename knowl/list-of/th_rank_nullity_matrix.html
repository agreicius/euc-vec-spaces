Corollary 4.5.13. Rank-nullity for matrices.

Let \(A\) be an \(m\times n\) matrix.
  1. Column and row space dimension.
    \begin{equation} \dim \CS A=\dim \RS A\text{.}\tag{4.5.4} \end{equation}
  2. Rank-nullity.
    \begin{align} n \amp= \nullity A+\rank A \tag{4.5.5}\\ \amp =\dim\NS A+\dim\CS A \tag{4.5.6}\\ \amp =\dim\NS A+\dim\RS A \text{.}\tag{4.5.7} \end{align}
